Few concerns about ice cream

Disadvantages

  • Ice cream is high in sugar, which can lead to weight gain and health issues.
  • It can contain high levels of saturated fat, which can increase the risk of heart disease.
  • Eating too much ice cream can lead to stomachaches, cramps, and other digestive issues.
